About Dino Dental Kampala

Bringing professional, compassionate dental care to the heart of Katwe since our founding.

Who We Are

A Clinic Built on Passion for Your Smile

Dino Dental Kampala was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to make quality dental care accessible, affordable, and genuinely enjoyable for every person in Kampala and beyond.

Located at Delta House in Katwe — right opposite Total BMK — our clinic has grown into a trusted dental home for thousands of patients. From young children visiting for the first time to adults seeking advanced restorative care, we treat every patient like family.

We believe a healthy smile transforms lives. That's why we invest in our team, our environment, and our ongoing education — ensuring you receive care that meets international standards right here in Kampala.
